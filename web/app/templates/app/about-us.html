Who We Are

Adwait Engineering is a distinguished provider of comprehensive Industrial Safety Equipment and Accessories (Safety & PPE), Electrical-Wire and Cables, Industrial Power Tools and Machinery, Office Stationery & Supplies, Material Handling Equipment, Spares, Aerial Work Platform, Packaging Material, Customized Solutions and Rental Services.

We are specializing in Material Handling Equipment and Consumables for B2B sectors. Established in 2024, we have solidified our position as a leader in optimizing industrial processes through innovative automation technologies.

Mission Statement

Our mission at Adwait Engineering is to provide complete industrial solutions that enhance Material Handling and streamline the supply of consumables for our B2B partners, contributing to their operational efficiency and growth.

Vision Statement

We envision becoming the preferred global partner for B2B industries seeking cutting-edge automation solutions for Material Handling and consumables, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer-centric approach.
